Privacy Policy

Barrel Proof - Whiskey Collection Tracker

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Barrel Proof ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Barrel Proof mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Whiskey Collection Data: When you add bottles to your collection, we store information you provide such as bottle name, distillery, type, age, ABV%, proof, price, photos, tasting notes, and ratings. This data is stored locally on your device.
  • Contributed Whiskeys: If you choose to contribute whiskeys to our shared database, we collect your name, email address, and the whiskey information you submit.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you provide.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect device-specific information such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you access and use the App, including your interactions with the App's features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our App
  • To allow you to track and manage your whiskey collection
  • To enable the shared whiskey database feature
  • To notify you about changes to our App
  • To provide customer support
  • To monitor the usage of our App

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your whiskey collection data is stored locally on your device using the browser's local storage. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account, your collection data may be synced to our secure cloud storage (Supabase) to allow you to access your collection across multiple devices. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Shared Database: Whiskeys you contribute to the shared database become publicly available and cannot be deleted, as they may have been accessed by other users.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect and process data:

  • Supabase: We use Supabase for cloud data storage and authentication. Their privacy policy applies to data stored with them.
  • GitHub Pages: Our App is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub's privacy policy applies to hosting services.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to delete your personal data
  • The right to object to or restrict processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 21 (or the legal drinking age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
